# 📄 Business Requirements

## Project Title

HR Analytics Dashboard

---

## Background

The Human Resources department requires an interactive reporting solution to monitor workforce performance, employee demographics, and attrition trends. The objective is to replace static reports with a dynamic dashboard that enables faster and more informed decision-making.

---

## Business Problem

The organization lacks a centralized dashboard for tracking key HR metrics. Decision-makers need visibility into workforce composition, employee turnover, and factors that may contribute to attrition.

---

## Project Objectives

The dashboard aims to:

- Monitor overall workforce size.
- Track employee attrition across departments and job roles.
- Analyze employee demographics.
- Evaluate workforce characteristics such as education, business travel, and work-life balance.
- Support data-driven HR planning and retention strategies.

---

## Key Business Questions

- How many employees currently work in the organization?
- What is the overall attrition rate?
- Which departments experience the highest attrition?
- Which job roles have the highest turnover?
- Does overtime appear to influence attrition?
- How is the workforce distributed by age, education, and job level?
- What are the patterns in work-life balance and job satisfaction?

---

## Stakeholders

- HR Managers
- HR Business Partners
- Department Managers
- Executive Leadership
- Business Analysts

---

## Success Criteria

The project will be considered successful if it:

- Presents HR metrics through interactive dashboards.
- Enables users to filter data dynamically.
- Supports business decision-making through clear visualizations.
- Improves visibility into employee attrition trends.
- Delivers a consistent and intuitive user experience.
