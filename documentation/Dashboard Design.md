# 🎨 Dashboard Design

## Overview

The HR Analytics Dashboard was designed to provide executives and HR professionals with an intuitive, interactive, and visually consistent reporting experience. The dashboard follows modern Business Intelligence design principles, emphasizing clarity, usability, and actionable insights.

---

## Design Principles

The dashboard was built using the following principles:

- Simplicity over complexity
- Consistent layout across all pages
- Clear visual hierarchy
- Business-focused storytelling
- Interactive exploration through slicers and tooltips
- Consistent color palette

---

# Dashboard Structure

The report consists of three pages.

## 1. Executive Overview

### Purpose

Provide senior management with a high-level summary of the workforce.

### KPIs

- Total Employees
- Current Employees
- Employees Left
- Attrition Rate
- Average Monthly Income

### Visuals

- Workforce Distribution by Job Role
- Employees by Age Band
- Employees Left by Department
- Attrition by Gender

---

## 2. Workforce Analysis

### Purpose

Analyze workforce demographics and employee characteristics.

### KPIs

- Total Employees
- Average Monthly Income
- Average Age
- Average Performance Rating
- Average Job Satisfaction

### Visuals

- Employees by Education Field
- Employees by Job Level
- Work-Life Balance
- Job Satisfaction
- Business Travel

---

## 3. Attrition Analysis

### Purpose

Identify employee turnover patterns and highlight potential drivers of attrition.

### KPIs

- Total Employees
- Employees Left
- Attrition Rate
- Average Age (Employees Left)
- Average Monthly Income (Employees Left)

### Visuals

- Employees Left by Department
- Employees Left by Job Role
- Employees Left by Education Field
- Employees Left by Age Band
- Employees Left by Overtime
- Employees Left by Marital Status

---

# Navigation

Navigation buttons were added to allow users to move seamlessly between the three dashboard pages without relying on Power BI page tabs.

---

# Interactivity

The dashboard includes:

- Interactive slicers
- Cross-filtering between visuals
- Report page tooltips
- Dynamic KPI cards
- Custom sorting for categorical data

---

# Color Palette

The dashboard uses a consistent color scheme to improve readability and reinforce meaning:

- 🔵 Blue — Workforce metrics and general analysis
- 🔴 Orange/Red — Attrition-focused metrics and visuals
- ⚪ White — KPI cards and backgrounds
- ⚫ Dark Gray — Titles and labels

This approach helps users distinguish between overall workforce information and attrition-related insights at a glance.

---

# User Experience

To improve usability, the dashboard incorporates:

- Consistent spacing and alignment
- Standardized font sizes
- Logical visual placement
- Responsive filtering across pages
- Executive-friendly layout

---

# Design Outcome

The final dashboard provides a professional reporting experience that enables HR managers and executives to monitor workforce performance, identify attrition trends, and support strategic decision-making through interactive visual analytics.
